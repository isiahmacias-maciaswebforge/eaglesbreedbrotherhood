Mission Statement

To provide ongoing support and assistance to our communities and their families through service, compassion, and unity.

Vision Statement

To remain true to our values while helping as many people as possible and leaving a lasting positive impact on every community we serve.

What We Do

We are a family-oriented motorcycle club that organizes poker runs, bike nights, and charity rides that bring riders together and support families in need throughout our community.

Our Chapters:

We currently have two chapters: our Mother Chapter in St. Paris, Ohio, and the Miami County Chapter in Piqua, Ohio.

For those unfamiliar with motorcycle clubs, chapters are independent organizations that operate in a specific area. Each chapter has its own leadership and chain of command while riding under the same patch and upholding the same ideals.

While each chapter manages its own local activities and events, both chapters work together to support rides, gatherings, and charitable efforts throughout the community.
