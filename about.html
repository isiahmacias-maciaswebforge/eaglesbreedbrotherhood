Who We Are

Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC is a motorcycle club built on loyalty, respect, and the bond of brotherhood. Our members share a passion for riding and supporting one another both on and off the road.

What started as a small group of riders has grown into a strong brotherhood dedicated to community involvement, charity events, and representing our club with pride wherever we ride.

Our Chapters

Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC currently operates two chapters in Ohio. Each chapter represents the club in its own community while sharing the same values and commitment to brotherhood.

Mother Chapter: St. Paris, Ohio
Miami County Chapter: Piqua, Ohio

Our History

On July 9, 2022, Wildman, Reaper, Chief, and a close friend were participating in a charity ride. After the run, they found themselves at the Renaissance Festival — sharing food, laughter, fellowship, and the idea of starting a motorcycle club.

At the time, one of their riding brothers was battling cancer. They wanted their riding to have purpose — something meaningful that could make a difference and create a lasting legacy.

All they needed was a name…

Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC was born.

Founded in 2022, the club has grown from a small group of passionate riders into a respected organization. The Miami County Chapter was established in 2024 to unite riders in the area, creating a space for fellowship while giving back to the community. Since then, the club has organized charity rides, supported local causes, and built a legacy of loyalty, brotherhood, and service across both chapters.

The Sisterhood

Our Sisterhood is the heart behind the iron. These incredible women support our mission, our events, and our brotherhood with strength, love, and dedication. From creating raffle baskets and selling 50/50 tickets to helping organize and set up poker runs, the Sisterhood plays a vital role in everything we do.

Our Values

Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC is built on a foundation of values that guide how we ride, how we represent our club, and how we treat the people around us. These principles are what hold the brotherhood together and ensure the club continues to grow with strength and respect.

Brotherhood – Our members stand beside one another on and off the road. The bond between brothers is the heart of our club.

Respect – Respect for our members, our community, and the traditions that define motorcycle club culture.

Loyalty – Loyalty to the club and to the brothers who wear its colors. The strength of the club depends on the dedication of its members.

Community – Giving back is an important part of what we do. Through charity rides and local events, we work to support the communities we ride in.

Leadership plays an important role in keeping the brotherhood strong. Our President helps guide the club, represent our values, and ensure that Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC continues to grow while staying true to the principles it was founded on.

Wildman, President of Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC, has been instrumental in shaping the direction of the club and building the brotherhood that members proudly represent today. Through dedication, leadership, and a passion for the road, he continues to help guide the club forward.
