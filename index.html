Welcome to the official website of Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC. We are a registered 501c-3 nonprofit motorcycle club dedicated to brotherhood, camaraderie, community service, and the freedom of the open road.

We proudly support local initiatives, host charitable events, and bring together riders who share a passion for making a positive impact in the communities we serve.

Mission Statement

To provide ongoing support and assistance to our communities and their families through service, compassion, and unity.

Vision Statement

To remain true to our values while helping as many people as possible and leaving a lasting positive impact on every community we serve.

How to Become
a Member

If you are interested in becoming a member, please reach out using the contact information at the bottom of the page.

Membership begins with the journey of becoming a Hang-Around. To do so, you will meet with several officers and members of the club. If you decide to move forward, you will begin showing the club who you are and whether you are a good fit for our brotherhood. This period serves as the first step toward becoming a full-patch member.

As a Hang-Around, no dues are required and nothing is mandatory. This is simply an opportunity for both you and the club to get to know each other. We promise — this isn’t TV.

Our History

On July 9, 2022, Wildman, Reaper, Chief, and a close friend were participating in a charity ride. After the run, they found themselves at the Renaissance Festival — sharing food, laughter, fellowship, and the idea of starting a motorcycle club.

At the time, one of their riding brothers was battling cancer. They wanted their riding to have purpose — something meaningful that could make a difference and create a lasting legacy.

All they needed was a name…

Eagles Breed Brotherhood MC was born.

Founded in 2022, the club has grown from a small group of passionate riders into a respected organization. The Miami County Chapter was established in 2024 to unite riders in the area, creating a space for fellowship while giving back to the community. Since then, the club has organized charity rides, supported local causes, and built a legacy of loyalty, brotherhood, and service across both chapters.

The Sisterhood

Our Sisterhood is the heart behind the iron. These incredible women support our mission, our events, and our brotherhood with strength, love, and dedication. From creating raffle baskets and selling 50/50 tickets to helping organize and set up poker runs, the Sisterhood plays a vital role in everything we do.
