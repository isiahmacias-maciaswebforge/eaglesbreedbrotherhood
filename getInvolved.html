How to Become a Member

If you are interested in becoming a member of Eagles Breed Brotherhood, the first step is simply reaching out. You can contact us using the information provided at the bottom of this page, and one of our members will connect with you.

Membership begins with the journey of becoming a Hang-Around. During this stage, you will meet several officers and members of the club, spend time around the brotherhood, and begin building relationships. If you choose to continue forward, this is your opportunity to show the club who you are and whether you are a good fit for our values and culture.

The Hang-Around stage is an important first step toward becoming a full-patch member. It allows both you and the club to get to know one another naturally and see if the brotherhood is the right place for you.

As a Hang-Around, there are no dues and nothing is mandatory. The goal is simply to spend time with the club and learn what Eagles Breed Brotherhood is all about. This is a real brotherhood built on respect, loyalty, and shared values — and we want everyone involved to know exactly what they’re becoming part of.
